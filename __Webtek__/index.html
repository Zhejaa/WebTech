What is Web Systems and Technology

This is a course that covers the design, implementation and testing of web-based applications and social software, and the incorporation of a variety of digital media into these applications. Students are exposed to a range of web technologies, both client-side and server-side.

Importance of Web Systems and Technology

As IT students, to keep up with the fast pace of improvement of the technologies world wide, it is crucial for each and every one of us to learn about Web Systems and Technology.
